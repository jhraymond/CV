DFA - GrassRoots Organization

Intent

Our intent is to help those needy peoples who have been decimated unwillingly by the relenetless march of corporate farms and the necessity that they purchase patented annual seeds.



Acute Goals

We wish to provide those with poor soils better and more proper treated soils based on their geographical location. We do this by treating soils with local microbes.



Chronic Goals

Our follow up plan gives us cause to monitor the efficacy of our long term soil treatment projects.



Future Soils

This is where we help people cultivate the rich types of soils found the world over. We must soon embark on a excurion to the Antarctic to extract those types of soils that have not seen the light of day since before the dinosaurs roamed the Earth.
